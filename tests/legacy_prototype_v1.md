# Legacy Prototype v1

> **目标**: 让玩家在第二世结束时，100%想开启第三世。
> **时长**: 30分钟
> **核心验证**: Legacy Loop（行动→遗产→死亡→后果→再行动）

---

## PROLOGUE

You close your eyes.

When you open them, you are small. Cold. Hungry.

A woman's voice: "This one's a fighter. Born during the frost."

You are in a village called Luoxia. The year is unknown. The season is winter.

You have no name yet.

---

## LIFE 1: THE UNKNOWN

### Age 6 — THE BEGINNING

You survive by begging at the inn gate. The innkeeper, Old Chen, sometimes gives you leftover buns.

**STATUS**:
- Strength: 2 | Agility: 3 | Comprehension: 4 | Charisma: 5
- Realm: None
- Hunger: 7/10
- Coins: 0

### Age 8 — THE ENCOUNTER

A man in black robes passes by. He smells of incense and steel.

He is **Zhang Tieshi**, a wandering swordsman. He drops a copper coin.

**CHOICE**:
- **A**: Bow and say "Thank you, master."
- **B**: Pick up the coin silently.
- **C**: Push the coin back. "I don't need charity."

---

*(We follow Path A for this prototype)*

#### PATH A — "Thank you, master."

Zhang Tieshi pauses. "Polite. That's rare."

He returns three days later. He watches you for an hour, then says:

"Come with me. I'll teach you to hold a sword."

You follow him to the Iron Gate Sect.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Charisma +1
- New relationship: Zhang Tieshi (master, trust: 30)
- New location: Iron Gate Sect

### Age 12 — THE TEST

Zhang Tieshi says: "I'm leaving this village. I won't come back."

**CHOICE**:
- **A1**: "Take me with you."
- **A2**: "Thank you for everything, master."

---

*(We follow A1)*

"Take me with you."

Zhang Tieshi sighs. "The road is dangerous."

"I don't care."

He looks at you for a long time. Then:

"Fine. But you carry your own weight."

You leave Luoxia Village.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Location: On the road
- Relationship: Zhang Tieshi (master, trust: 50)
- New skill: Basic Swordsmanship (Level 1)

### Age 15 — THE BANDITS

On the road, bandits attack. Five of them.

You kill two. Zhang Tieshi kills three.

He bandages your wound. "I'm proud of you."

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Strength +2
- Agility +1
- Permanent scar on arm
- Zhang Tieshi (master, trust: 80)

### Age 18 — THE SECRET

You arrive at Iron Gate Sect. The Sect Master, **Li Kui**, is suspicious.

You train for two years. You reach **Martial Apprentice Level 3**.

Then one night, you overhear a conversation:

Li Kui wants Zhang Tieshi dead. Because Zhang Tieshi knows a secret about Li Kui's past — a murder.

**CHOICE**:
- **D**: Tell Zhang Tieshi immediately.
- **E**: Stay silent and wait.
- **F**: Confront Li Kui directly.

---

*(We follow D)*

#### PATH D — Tell Zhang Tieshi

You rush to Zhang Tieshi's room. "Master, Li Kui wants to kill you."

Zhang Tieshi's face darkens. "I know."

"You KNOW?"

"I've known for years. That's why I came back — to end this."

He draws his sword. "Stay behind me."

A battle erupts. Zhang Tieshi defeats Li Kui, but is fatally wounded.

His last words: "Wu Ming... the Iron Gate Sect... is yours now."

You are 20 years old. You are the new Sect Master.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Realm: Martial Apprentice Level 5
- Position: Sect Master of Iron Gate Sect
- Relationship: Zhang Tieshi (DEAD — trust: 100, memory: legendary)
- Reputation: +50

### Age 25 — THE DECISION

Five years as Sect Master. The sect grows.

But then — a plague sweeps through the sect. Your best disciple, **Chen Yu**, dies.

You bury him next to Zhang Tieshi's grave.

**CHOICE** (Legacy Decision):
- **G**: Close the sect. "I can't bear to lose anyone else."
- **H**: Rebuild stronger. "Chen Yu would want me to continue."
- **I**: Seek revenge on the Poison Snake Gang (who you suspect caused the plague).

---

*(We follow H — Rebuild Stronger)*

#### PATH H — Rebuild Stronger

You train new disciples. You build a school within the sect. You teach what Zhang Tieshi taught you.

The sect grows to 60 disciples.

**LEGACY CREATED**: "The disciple who rebuilt Iron Gate Sect after the plague"

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Sect members: 60
- Reputation: +30
- Legacy: Physical (school built), Social (sect restored), Legend (story of rebuilding)

### Age 30 — THE FALL

The Poison Snake Gang attacks.

You fight. You kill their lieutenant. But there are too many.

Your sect falls. You barely escape with 12 survivors.

You are wounded. Bleeding. The winter wind cuts through your robes.

You collapse in the snow outside Luoxia Village.

An old woman finds you. She says:

"You look like someone I knew. A beggar boy. Long ago."

You try to speak. Blood comes out instead.

Your last thought: *I hope the school survives.*

**DEATH**.

---

## REINCARNATION

### The Space Between Lives

You float in darkness. A voice speaks:

**"Your life has ended. But your story is not over."**

**"Your legacy has been recorded."**

You see three things:

1. **The school you built** — still standing, but damaged
2. **The 12 survivors** — scattered, some turned to banditry
3. **Zhang Tieshi's grave** — flowers still placed there annually

**"Choose which memory to carry into your next life."**

You see three fragments:

1. **Zhang Tieshi's smile** — "I'm proud of you." (Strength +2, Loyalty +20)
2. **The fall of Iron Gate Sect** — Fire, screams, death. (Comprehension +3, Caution +30)
3. **The school you built** — Children learning, laughing. (Charisma +2, Legacy +20)

**CHOICE**: Pick ONE memory to keep.

---

#### If you choose Fragment 3 — The school you built

You are born again.

This time, in a different village. Your father is a blacksmith.

You are named **Li Feng**.

You are 6 years old. You feel... something. A warmth in your chest.

A vision: *Children laughing in a school. A building you built. A life you lived.*

You don't know who you were. But you know you built something that mattered.

---

## LIFE 2: THE LEGACY

### Age 6 — THE DISCOVERY

Your father teaches you to hammer iron. You're strong for your age.

**STATUS**:
- Strength: 4 (+2 from memory)
- Agility: 3
- Comprehension: 3
- Charisma: 4
- Legacy memory: The school you built

### Age 10 — THE PILGRIMAGE

You feel drawn to a place you've never been. A village called Luoxia.

You walk there. Three days on foot.

When you arrive, you see:

**A building.**

It's old. Weathered. But still standing.

Children are laughing inside.

A teacher — an old man — comes out.

"Welcome, young one. Are you here to learn?"

You look at the building. Something triggers in your memory.

"I... I think I built this."

The teacher laughs. "You're only ten years old. This building was built by the founder of Iron Gate Sect, over a hundred years ago."

He points to a plaque above the door:

**「无名学堂」**

Below it, a smaller inscription:

**「林无名 创立」**

Your heart stops.

*Lin Wuming.*

*That was your name.*

*In your previous life.*

**LEGACY TRIGGERED**: You found the school you built in your previous life.

### Age 10 — THE LEGACY CHANGES

You walk through the school. Everything is familiar.

But something is wrong.

The teacher tells you the history:

"Our founder, Lin Wuming, rebuilt Iron Gate Sect after the great plague. He built this school to teach children. But after he died, the sect fell. The school survived, but... it's not what it used to be."

"What happened?"

"The sect split into three factions. They've been fighting for a hundred years. The school... we're just surviving."

**LEGACY CONSEQUENCE**: Your school survived, but your sect fell. Your legacy is incomplete.

**CHOICE**:
- **J**: "I want to help rebuild the school."
- **K**: "I want to find out what happened to the sect."
- **L**: "I want to understand who I was."

---

*(We follow J)*

#### PATH J — Help rebuild the school

You start teaching children. You're good at it.

The old teacher says: "You have a gift. It's like you've done this before."

"I think I have."

Over the years, you transform the school. You add new buildings. You create a curriculum.

By age 20, the school has 50 students. The best school in the region.

**LEGACY EVOLUTION**: Your school from Life 1 has been rebuilt and improved.

### Age 25 — THE CONFRONTATION

The three factions of Iron Gate Sect learn about your school.

The **Orthodox Faction** sends an envoy: "Your school teaches our sect's techniques without permission."

The **Reform Faction** sends a spy: "We want to learn your methods."

The **Revenge Faction** sends a threat: "You're the descendant of our enemy. We'll destroy your school."

**CHOICE**:
- **M**: Fight the Revenge Faction.
- **N**: Negotiate with all three factions.
- **O**: Abandon the school and flee.

---

*(We follow N)*

#### PATH N — Negotiate

You invite all three factions to the school.

You stand before them and say:

"I know who I was. I was Lin Wuming. I built Iron Gate Sect. I built this school. And I watched it all fall apart."

"You're the reincarnation of Lin Wuming?" the Orthodox leader gasps.

"Yes. And I'm here to tell you: the fighting has to stop. Lin Wuming didn't build this school so you could tear each other apart."

"You don't know what the original Lin Wuming would have wanted."

"I know exactly what he would have wanted. He would have wanted his legacy to mean something."

Silence.

Then, the Reform Faction leader speaks: "What do you propose?"

"A alliance. All three factions. One sect. One school."

The negotiation takes three months. But you succeed.

**LEGACY COMPLETE**: You rebuilt what you created in Life 1. The sect is whole again.

### Age 30 — THE CHOICE

You are 30 years old. The sect is united. The school is thriving.

But you feel... empty.

You walk to Zhang Tieshi's grave. You place flowers.

"I built what you wanted, master. But I still don't know why I came back."

Then you hear a voice behind you:

"You came back to finish what you started."

You turn. An old woman stands there.

She is **Old Wang**. A former disciple of Iron Gate Sect. She knew Zhang Tieshi. She knew Lin Wuming.

"You... you remember me?"

"I've been waiting for you. For a hundred years."

She holds out a letter. "Zhang Tieshi left this for you. He knew you'd come back."

You open the letter. It reads:

*"Wu Ming — if you're reading this, you've returned. I'm proud of you. But I need to tell you something: I didn't die from the battle. Li Kui poisoned me before the fight. I knew I was dying. That's why I gave you the sect. I wanted you to have something to protect. I'm sorry I couldn't tell you the truth. But now you know. Live well. — Zhang Tieshi"*

You cry.

The letter falls to the ground.

Old Wang says: "There's more. Zhang Tieshi had a daughter. She survived. She has descendants. They're still alive."

**CHOICE** (The final choice of Life 2):
- **P**: "Take me to them."
- **Q**: "No. I've done enough. Let them live in peace."
- **R**: "Write to them. Tell them the truth."

---

*(We follow P)*

#### PATH P — Take me to them

Old Wang leads you to a small village.

There, you meet **Lin Xiaoyu**, the great-great-great-granddaughter of Zhang Tieshi.

She looks at you. "You look like someone my grandmother described."

"Who?"

"A man named Lin Wuming. She said he was the bravest person she ever knew."

You smile. "I think I knew him."

**LEGACY TRIGGERED**: You found Zhang Tieshi's descendants. The circle is complete.

### Age 35 — THE END

You die peacefully, surrounded by your students and Zhang Tieshi's descendants.

Your last thought: *I finished what I started.*

**DEATH**.

---

## REINCARNATION 2

### The Space Between Lives

You float in darkness. A voice speaks:

**"Your life has ended. But your story continues."**

**"Your legacy has evolved."**

You see three things:

1. **The school** — now a university, teaching hundreds
2. **The united sect** — the strongest it's been in a century
3. **Zhang Tieshi's descendants** — living in peace, knowing the truth

**"What will you leave behind this time?"**

---

## END OF PROTOTYPE

### What just happened:

1. **Life 1**: You created a school and rebuilt a sect
2. **Reincarnation 1**: You found your school, now 100 years old
3. **Life 2**: You rebuilt your legacy, united the sect, found Zhang Tieshi's descendants
4. **Reincarnation 2**: You see the full impact of your actions

### Legacy Loop Demonstrated:

```
Life 1: Create school → Die
        ↓
Life 2: Find school → Rebuild → Unite sect → Find descendants → Die
        ↓
Life 3: ??? (What will you change next?)
```

### Core Question:

> **After playing this prototype, would you want to start a third life?**

If yes → Legacy Loop works.
If no → the design needs rethinking.

### Design Notes:

- **Total playtime**: ~30 minutes
- **Deaths**: 2 (planned)
- **Reincarnations**: 2
- **Legacy triggers**: 3 (school discovery, sect rebuilding, descendant finding)
- **Emotional arc**: Hope → Growth → Legacy → Death → Discovery → Rebuilding → Completion → Death → What's next?

### What makes this stronger than Paper Prototype v0:

1. **You see your legacy change** — the school survived but degraded
2. **You can rebuild** — not just witness, but act
3. **You find descendants** — personal connection across lives
4. **You discover truth** — Zhang Tieshi's letter changes everything
5. **The circle completes** — you finish what you started
