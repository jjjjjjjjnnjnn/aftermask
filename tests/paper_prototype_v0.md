# 百世江湖 Paper Prototype v0

> **Instructions**: Read through this prototype as if you were playing the game. At each "CHOICE" point, pick an option and follow the corresponding path. The entire experience should take about 15 minutes.

---

## PROLOGUE

You close your eyes.

When you open them, you are small. Cold. Hungry.

A woman's voice: "This one's a fighter. Born during the frost. Bad omen."

You are in a village called Luoxia. The year is unknown. The season is winter.

You have no name yet.

---

## LIFE 1: THE BEGGAR

### Age 6

You survive by begging at the inn gate. The innkeeper, Old Chen, sometimes gives you leftover buns.

**STATUS**:
- Strength: 2 | Agility: 3 | Comprehension: 4 | Charisma: 5
- Realm: None
- Hunger: 7/10 (dangerous)
- Coins: 0

### Age 8 — THE ENCOUNTER

A man in black robes passes by. He smells of incense and steel. He stops, looks at you, and says:

"You have sharp eyes for a beggar."

He is **Zhang Tieshi**, a wandering swordsman. He drops a copper coin.

**CHOICE**:
- **A**: Pick up the coin and bow. ("Thank you, master.")
- **B**: Pick up the coin but say nothing.
- **C**: Push the coin back toward him. ("I don't need charity.")

---

#### PATH A — "Thank you, master."

Zhang Tieshi pauses. "Polite. That's rare."

He walks away. But three days later, he returns. He watches you for an hour, then says:

"Come with me. I'll teach you to hold a sword."

You follow him to the Iron Gate Sect. You become his **informal disciple** — not a real student, just a boy who carries his sword case.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Charisma +1 (politeness noticed)
- New relationship: Zhang Tieshi (master, trust: 30)
- New location: Iron Gate Sect

---

#### PATH B — Pick up the coin silently

Zhang Tieshi frowns. "No manners." He walks away.

He does not return.

But a week later, a gang of older beggars beats you for "trespassing on their territory." You lose 3 coins worth of food.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Hunger: 9/10 (critical)
- No new relationship
- Injury: bruised ribs (-1 agility for 7 days)

---

#### PATH C — Push the coin back

Zhang Tieshi raises an eyebrow. "Interesting."

He crouches down. "What's your name?"

"I don't have one."

"Then I'll give you one. **Wu Ming**. Nameless."

He doesn't take you as a disciple. But he tells the innkeeper: "Feed this boy. I'll pay."

The innkeeper reluctantly agrees. You eat your first full meal in weeks.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Hunger: 3/10 (recovered)
- Charisma +2 (unusual behavior noticed)
- Zhang Tieshi (acquaintance, trust: 10)
- New name: Wu Ming

---

### Age 12 — THE TEST

Zhang Tieshi returns. He says:

"I'm leaving this village. I won't come back."

**CHOICE** (if you followed Path A):
- **A1**: "Take me with you."
- **A2**: "Thank you for everything, master."

**CHOICE** (if you followed Path B):
- **B1**: Chase after him.
- **B2**: Stay and beg.

**CHOICE** (if you followed Path C):
- **C1**: "Where are you going?"
- **C2**: Say nothing.

---

*(For this prototype, we'll follow PATH A, Choice A1)*

#### PATH A1 — "Take me with you."

Zhang Tieshi sighs. "The road is dangerous."

"I don't care."

He looks at you for a long time. Then:

"Fine. But you carry your own weight."

You leave Luoxia Village. You are 12 years old. You have nothing but the clothes on your back.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Location: On the road
- Relationship: Zhang Tieshi (master, trust: 50)
- New skill: Basic Swordsmanship (Level 1)

---

### Age 15 — THE BANDITS

On the road to Qingfeng Town, bandits attack.

There are five of them. Zhang Tieshi fights two. The rest charge at you.

**COMBAT**:
- Your attack: 8 (strength + weapon)
- Bandit HP: 10 each
- Your HP: 30

You kill two bandits. The third slashes your arm. Pain. Blood.

Zhang Tieshi finishes his opponents and rushes to your side.

"You're bleeding."

"I know."

He tears his sleeve and bandages your wound. His hands are gentle.

"I'm proud of you," he says.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Strength +2 (combat experience)
- Agility +1
- Injury: arm wound (permanent scar)
- Zhang Tieshi (master, trust: 80)
- **LEGEND CREATED**: "The boy who killed two bandits at age 15" — witnesses will remember

---

### Age 18 — THE BETRAYAL

You arrive at Iron Gate Sect. Zhang Tieshi introduces you as his disciple.

But the Sect Master, **Li Kui**, is suspicious.

"You're a beggar's child. Why should the Iron Gate Sect accept you?"

Zhang Tieshi defends you. Li Kui relents — grudgingly.

You train for two years. You reach **Martial Apprentice Level 3**.

Then one night, you overhear a conversation:

Li Kui wants Zhang Tieshi dead. Because Zhang Tieshi knows a secret about Li Kui's past — a murder.

**CHOICE**:
- **D**: Tell Zhang Tieshi immediately.
- **E**: Stay silent and wait.
- **F**: Confront Li Kui directly.

---

#### PATH D — Tell Zhang Tieshi

You rush to Zhang Tieshi's room. "Master, Li Kui wants to kill you."

Zhang Tieshi's face darkens. "I know."

"You KNOW?"

"I've known for years. That's why I came back — to end this."

He draws his sword. "Stay behind me."

A battle erupts. Zhang Tieshi defeats Li Kui, but is fatally wounded.

His last words: "Wu Ming... the Iron Gate Sect... is yours now."

You are 20 years old. You are the new Sect Master.

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Realm: Martial Apprentice Level 5 (promoted)
- Position: Sect Master of Iron Gate Sect
- Relationship: Zhang Tieshi (DEAD — trust: 100, memory: legendary)
- Reputation: +50 (avenged master)
- **WORLD EVENT**: "Zhang Tieshi's disciple took control of Iron Gate Sect" — this will be remembered

---

### Age 25 — THE DECLINE

Five years as Sect Master. The sect grows. You train disciples. You make alliances.

But you make an enemy: **The Poison Snake Gang**. Their leader, **Xiao Mei**, accuses you of stealing their territory.

War is coming.

You prepare. But then — a plague sweeps through the sect. Your best disciple, **Chen Yu**, dies.

You bury him next to Zhang Tieshi's grave.

That night, you drink alone. You think about Zhang Tieshi's last words.

"The Iron Gate Sect is yours now."

Is this what he wanted?

**STATUS CHANGE**:
- Age: 25
- Sect members: 45
- Resources: Low (plague depleted stores)
- Enemy: Poison Snake Gang (hostile)
- Relationship: Chen Yu (DEAD — trust: 90, memory: painful)

---

### Age 30 — THE FALL

The Poison Snake Gang attacks.

You fight. You kill their lieutenant. But there are too many.

Your sect falls. You barely escape with 12 survivors.

You are wounded. Bleeding. The winter wind cuts through your robes.

You collapse in the snow outside Luoxia Village — the village where you were born.

An old woman finds you. She says:

"You look like someone I knew. A beggar boy. Long ago."

You try to speak. Blood comes out instead.

Your last thought: *I never learned Zhang Tieshi's secret.*

**DEATH**.

---

## REINCARNATION

### The Space Between Lives

You float in darkness. A voice speaks:

**"Your life has ended. But your story is not over."**

**"Choose which memory to carry into your next life."**

You see three fragments:

1. **Zhang Tieshi's smile** — "I'm proud of you." (Strength +2, Loyalty +20)
2. **The fall of Iron Gate Sect** — Fire, screams, death. (Comprehension +3, Caution +30)
3. **Chen Yu's grave** — Your best student, dead before his time. (Charisma +2, Grief +20)

**CHOICE**: Pick ONE memory to keep.

---

#### If you choose Fragment 1 — Zhang Tieshi's smile

You are born again.

This time, in a different village. Your father is a blacksmith. Your mother is a weaver.

You are named **Li Feng**.

You are 6 years old. You feel... something. A warmth in your chest. A memory that isn't yours.

A man's voice: *"I'm proud of you."*

You don't know who said it. But you know it was important.

---

## LIFE 2: THE BLACKSMITH'S SON

### Age 6

Your father teaches you to hammer iron. You're strong for your age.

**STATUS**:
- Strength: 4 (+2 from memory)
- Agility: 3
- Comprehension: 3
- Charisma: 4
- Loyalty: 70 (+20 from memory)
- Memory fragment: Zhang Tieshi's smile

### Age 10 — THE RECOGNITION

A wandering swordsman visits the village. He stops at your father's forge.

You look up.

He has a scar on his left hand. He smells of incense and steel.

He looks at you. His eyes widen.

"...Wu Ming?"

You don't understand. "My name is Li Feng."

He stares at you for a long time. Then he shakes his head.

"No. Of course not. He died 20 years ago."

But he keeps looking at you. His hands are trembling.

"You look exactly like him."

**WORLD MEMORY TRIGGERED**: The swordsman is **Old Wang**, a former disciple of Iron Gate Sect. He knew Zhang Tieshi. He knew Wu Ming.

He recognizes something in you.

**CHOICE**:
- **G**: "Who was Wu Ming?"
- **H**: "You're mistaken. I'm just a blacksmith's son."
- **I**: Say nothing.

---

*(This is where the prototype ends. The experience loops.)*

## END OF PROTOTYPE

### What just happened:

1. **Grew up** — from beggar to sect master
2. **Made choices** — each shaped the story
3. **Faced consequences** — betrayal, loss, war
4. **Died** — and it felt meaningful
5. **Reincarnated** — with a fragment of the past
6. **World remembered** — an NPC recognized the old you

### Design Notes:

- **Total playtime**: ~15 minutes
- **Deaths**: 1 (planned)
- **Reincarnations**: 1
- **World memory triggers**: 1 (Old Wang recognizing Wu Ming)
- **Emotional arc**: Hope → Growth → Pride → Loss → Death → Rebirth → Recognition

### Core Question:

> **After playing this prototype, would you want to start a third life?**

If yes → the core loop works.
If no → the design needs rethinking.

---

## NEXT ITERATION PROMPTS

For the next version, we need:

1. **More choices per life** — 3-5 decision points, not 2
2. **Multiple death causes** — not just "war"
3. **NPC relationship tracking** — show how relationships evolve
4. **World state changes** — what happened to Iron Gate Sect after you died?
5. **Third life preview** — let the player see the consequences of their choices
